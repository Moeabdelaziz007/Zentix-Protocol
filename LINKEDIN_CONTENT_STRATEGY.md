## IP Protection Guidelines

### What NOT to Share
1. **Detailed Technical Architecture**: Avoid revealing specific implementation details
2. **Smart Contract Logic**: Don't share code snippets or contract structures
3. **Monetization Formulas**: Keep specific revenue models confidential
4. **Competitive Advantages**: Don't explicitly state what makes you unique vs. competitors
5. **Roadmap Details**: Avoid sharing specific timelines or upcoming features

### What TO Share
1. **High-Level Concepts**: General approaches and philosophies
2. **Industry Insights**: Analysis of trends and opportunities
3. **Learning Experiences**: Lessons from development process
4. **Vision and Future**: Where the space is heading
5. **Problem-Solution Framework**: General approaches to challenges

### Content Framing Techniques
1. **Academic Style**: Frame as research and exploration rather than finished products
2. **Future-Focused**: Discuss possibilities rather than current capabilities
3. **Educational Approach**: Teach concepts rather than showcase specific implementations
4. **Collaborative Tone**: Invite others to build on ideas rather than claim ownership
5. **Question-Driven**: Pose questions rather than state definitive answers
